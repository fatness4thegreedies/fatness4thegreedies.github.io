fatness4...the greedies

Undoubtedly, the sounds of the 90s flicker through the finely braided song structures of these 3 gentlemen from Bremen, Northern Germany. They guide you through dense, driving guitar walls, sensitive vocal lines and oblique drum foundations to reduced emotional song passages to create sound images that take you on a journey. The new project „fatness4…the greedies“ was created after the death of their previous bands and Falko began writing new songs in 2020. Falko Brunswick guitars, bass, organ, backing vocals Axel Clausen drums, backing vocals Yasin Boranbay lead vocals We hope you‘ll like it and see you on a concert.
